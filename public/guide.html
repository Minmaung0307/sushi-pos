Dashboard

Inventory

Products

  • Click a product image (or cart icon) to see the detail card.
  • Track barcode, price, type, ingredients, instructions.

COGS

  • Add rows for daily costs and income; see profit in the table.
  • Totals auto-calc at the bottom; CSV export included.

Tasks

  • Drag any task across lanes (To do, In progress, Done).
  • Add/Edit/Delete as your role allows.

  • Users (manage roles within your permission level).
