Privacy & Data Policy

For the Sushi POS web app

Overview

Sushi POS is a local-first web app. Your data is kept in your browser storage by default. If you enable Cloud Sync in Settings, we store a copy in your Firebase Realtime Database under your authenticated account.

What we store

  • Local (browser): inventory, products, posts, tasks, COGS, theme, user list (app roles).
  • Cloud (optional): the same keys under /tenants/{uid}/kv/* in your Firebase RTDB when Sync is ON.
  • Auth: Email & password are handled by Firebase Authentication.

How we use your data

  • To render the app and keep your data across sessions/devices when Cloud Sync is ON.
  • To enforce access rules so users can only read/write their own tenant data.

Security

  • Access is controlled by Firebase Authentication.
  • Database Security Rules restrict access to auth.uid-scoped paths.
  • Always use HTTPS (Hosting provides this by default).

Retention & deletion

  • Local data stays in your browser until you clear it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data remains in your RTDB until you delete it or disable Sync and purge.

Your choices

  • Keep Sync OFF to stay fully local.
  • Turn Sync ON to use data across devices; you can turn it OFF any time.
  • Export or delete data from Firebase Console if you’re the project owner.
