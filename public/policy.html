Privacy & Data Policy

We keep things simple. Your data lives locally in your browser (LocalStorage). If you enable Cloud Sync in Settings and are logged in with Firebase, we mirror your data to your private Firebase Realtime Database.

What we store

  • Inventory, Products, Posts, Tasks, COGS, Users, and Theme.
  • Session info (your email, role) to keep you signed in.

Cloud Sync

When Cloud Sync is ON, we write your data to tenants/<your-uid>/kv/<key> in Firebase. Turn it OFF any time.

Security

  • Authentication provided by Firebase Auth.
  • Owner controls access; roles restrict actions (view/add/edit/delete).

Contact

Use the in-app Contact page to email the team.
