User Guide

Quick tips for managers & staff
Sign in & roles

Use your email and password to sign in. Admin/Manager can manage users from Settings → Users.

Inventory
  • Add items, adjust stock/threshold, and watch for low/critical highlights.
  • Export CSV anytime from the top-right of the Inventory page.
Products
  • Keep barcodes, ingredients, and instructions up to date.
  • Click a product thumbnail to open a detail preview.
COGS
  • Enter daily rows; totals and gross profit calculate automatically.
  • Dashboard shows same-month year-over-year (YoY) gross income.
  • Export CSV from the COGS page.
Tasks
  • Drag & drop tasks freely among To do, In progress, and Done, even when lanes are empty.
Cloud Sync (optional)
  • Turn ON in Settings to sync local data to your Firebase RTDB (private to your user UID).
  • Use Sync Now for a manual push when needed.
Theme

Switch mode and font size in Settings; changes apply instantly.
