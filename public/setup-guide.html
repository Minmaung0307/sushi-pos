3) Email (Contact page)

By default the Contact page opens your email client (mailto). To send via EmailJS:

  1. Create a free account at EmailJS.
  2. Create a service and template.
  3. In app.js, call emailjs.init('YOUR_PUBLIC_KEY') and set your service_id / template_id in the Contact handler.

4) Roles & Permissions

  • Admin: read, add, edit, delete.
  • Manager: read, add, edit.
  • Associate: read, add.
  • User: read only.

5) Deploy

  1. Host the index.html, js/app.js, and css/styles.css anywhere (Firebase Hosting, Netlify, Vercel, etc.).
  2. Make sure your Firebase auth/domain is allowed in your project settings (Authorized domains).
